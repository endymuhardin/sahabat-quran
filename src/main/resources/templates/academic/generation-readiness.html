Generation Process Information

What happens during generation:

  • Students are grouped by validated level assignments
  • Teachers are matched based on level assignments and availability
  • Classes are optimized for size, balance, and schedule conflicts
  • Multiple proposals may be generated for comparison

After generation you can:

  • Review and manually adjust class compositions
  • Transfer students between classes
  • Resolve conflicts and size violations
  • Approve the final class schedule
